Aprajita Foundation

About Us



The Aprajita Foundation is a non-profit organization based in Delhi, India, with a primary focus on women's empowerment and the promotion of art and culture. Founded by Soni Nilu Jha, a dedicated social activist, the foundation endeavors to create positive change in society by addressing various issues related to gender equality, education, skill development, and cultural preservation.

Soni Nilu Jha, the founder of Aprajita Foundation, is a social activist for women's rights and has dedicated her life to empowering women from all walks of life. With a strong belief in the transformative power of education and art, she established the foundation as a platform to support and uplift women, providing them with opportunities for growth, education, and economic independence.

The Aprajita Foundation operates through various initiatives, including skill development programs, educational workshops, awareness campaigns, and artistic endeavors. Through these efforts, the foundation aims to empower women to become self-reliant, confident, and active participants in their communities.

Furthermore, the foundation is deeply committed to promoting art and culture, recognizing the importance of preserving and celebrating India's rich cultural heritage. Through collaborations with artists, cultural institutions, and community organizations, the Aprajita Foundation works to foster appreciation for art and culture while also providing platforms for artists to showcase their talent and creativity.

Overall, the Aprajita Foundation, under the leadership of Soni Nilu Jha, continues to make significant strides in advancing its mission of women's empowerment and cultural enrichment, leaving a positive impact on society and inspiring positive change.
