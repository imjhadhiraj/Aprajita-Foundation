Our aim ?

Our aim is to promote art and culture in each and every corner of India. In parallel to this aim,
we also focus on women encouragement and empowerment. We have done many events which
mainly involved women. We have performed events in different places and we consist of a team varying
different age groups.

Art is the reason of our existence and it should be preserved...
